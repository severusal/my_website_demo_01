Shanghai Disneyland Park

Shanghai Disneyland Park (Chinese: 上海迪士尼乐园) is a theme park located in Pudong, Shanghai, China, that is part of the Shanghai Disney Resort.

Happy Valley Beijing

Happy Valley Beijing(Chinese: 北京欢乐谷) is an amusement park in Beijing built and operated by Beijing OCT (Chinese: 北京华侨城), which is part of the Overseas Chinese Town Company.

Universal Studios Beijing

Universal Studios Beijing (Chinese: 北京环球影城) is an upcoming Universal theme park in Beijing that will open in Spring 2021 as part of Universal Beijing Resort.
